The Rosewood State Rosas football team represents Rosewood State University in the sport of American football. The Rosas compete in the NCAA Football Bowl Subdivision and the Mid-Atlantic Conference. Rosewood State was one of the founding members of the Mid-Atlantic Conference, which was founded in 1913.

History

The Rosewood State football program began in 1913.
