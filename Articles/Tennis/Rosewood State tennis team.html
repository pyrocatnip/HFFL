Rosewood State Rosas Tennis team

The Rosewood State Rosas tennis team represents Rosewood State University in the sport of tennis. The Rosas compete in the Mid-Atlantic Conference. The program began in 2020 and was able to win a Collegiate Tennis National Championship in its first season.
