The 2020 Hyundai Open – Women's Singles tournament was the first annual edition of the women's singles event, which is a part of the Hyundai Open. The tournament is held at Frederick Douglass Stadium, and is operated by the United Tennis Federation. The tournament is a part of the UTF Tour, and is categorized as a 250 tournament, which is the loweest tier of professional tournments on the tour. The tournament had a draw of 128 with the participants split into four groups of 16. The tournemnt also included a Wild Card qualifying draw which saw 16 players face each other in individual 1 on 1 matches in order to win a spot in one of the eight sections of the main draw.

Venue

The Hyundai Open is currently held at Frederick Douglass Stadium, located in Douglass. The stadium is able to hold a maximum capacity of 28,000 as of 2020. The venue is located only 2 miles away from the UTF headquarters.
