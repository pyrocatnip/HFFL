List of Union City Red Devils Starting Quarterbacks

Seventeen have started at least one game for the Union City Red Devils since its founding in 2020. Only five of those quarterbacks have won at least one playoff game, with nine of them never starting in a playoff game during their time with the Red Devils. These players are listed in order of the date of each player's first start at quarterback for the Red Devils.
