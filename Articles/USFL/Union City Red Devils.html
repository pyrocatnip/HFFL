    • The Union City Red Devils are a professional American football team based in Union City, Maryland. The team was founded in 2020 and play in the United States Football League (USFL). The Red Devils compete in the United States Football League as a member of the Eastern Conference. The Red Devils play their home games in Leon Britton Stadium. The team is headquartered in Union City, Maryland. The team was one of the founding members of the USFL.
