List of current members of the United States House of Delegates

This article is about senators currently serving in the United States Congress. For a list of senators in U.S. state legislatures, see List of U.S. state senators

The United States House of Delegates consists of 112 delegates, two from each of the 52 states and two from the four territories. This includes all currrent delegates serving in the 135th United States Congress.
