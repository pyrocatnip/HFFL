    History of Morocco
    Amallah Emirate
    (769-1066)
    First Holy War

    The Amallah Emirate was a Christian kingdom located in modern-day Morocco. The Emirate was created in 769 following the end of the First Holy War, which was led by the Sheikdom of Sous.
