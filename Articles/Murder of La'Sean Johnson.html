The Murder of La'Sean Johnson refers an event that occurred on January 28, 2025, which resulted in the death of 22-year old college student La'Sean Johnson. The event ocurred in Georgetown, Douglass. Johnson was fatally shot and killed by Carl Williams, a member of the Metropolitan Police. Williams initially investigated Johnson as a suspect of a recent robbery in the Georgetown area, when Johnson who had just left class at Georgetown University was pulled over. Williams attempted to physically restrain Johnson, and was thrown on the ground. Johnson freed hismelf and tried to losen the grip of the officer Williams when he was shot six times in the back near his spinal cord. The whole event was recorded on video by Williams, and by bystanders in the area.

The video of Johnson's death quickly went viral, and protests began only three hours after the initial incident ocurred at 12:35 PM. The initial wave of protests lasted from January 29th until February 3rd, and led to officer Williams being placed on paid leave during an internal investiagtion. The family of Johnson would file a criminal lawsuit against Johnson on the 10th of February, the day after the internal investigation by the Metropolitan Police found no wrong doing on William's part.
