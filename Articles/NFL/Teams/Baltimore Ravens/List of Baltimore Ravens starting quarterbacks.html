List of Baltimore Ravens Starting Quarterbacks

Ninteen quarterbacks have started at least one game for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League. Eight of those quarterbacks have started in at least one playoff game for the Ravens. These players are listed in order of the date each player's first start at quarterback for the Ravens.

    Early History (1996-2007)

    The early history for the Ravens at the Quarterback was unstable, with sixteen quarterbacks starting at least one game for the Ravens from 1996 until 2007.

    Scott Odom (2007-2019)

    Scott Odom was the starting quarterback for the Ravens from 2007 until 2019. Odom attented Rosewood State University, where he won the Rose Bowl in 2004, as well as the Heisman Trophy in his senior season in 2005.

    Odom was selected in the second round of the 2007 NFL Draft by the Ravens, in the hopes that the Ravens could find stability at Quarterback.
