John Ambrose II is an American football quarterback for the Baltimore Ravens, of the National Football League. He was drafted with the last pick of the 2024 NFL draft and has played for the Ravens for his entire career. Ambrose II is the son of former Bills quarterback, and current Cleveland Browns Offensive Coordinator, John Ambrose. Ambrose II attended Rosewood State University.Ambrose won two Super Bowl MVP awards, 3 pro bowl nods, 2 First-Team All-Pro selections, 3 NFL MVP awards, 2 OPOY awards, and is currently ranked as the fourth greatest quarterback of all time. Ambrose was the 2024 Mr. Irrelevant, which is a joke award given to the last overall pick of each NFL draft.
