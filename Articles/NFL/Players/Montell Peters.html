Montell Peters is a professional American football quarterback for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. He was drafted with the 17th pick in the 7th round of the 2029 NFL draft by Tampa Bay. Peters attended the University of North Carolina, where he won First Team All-ACC honors in his junior season.

Professional Career

Tampa Bay Buccaneers

Peters was drafted in the 7th round of the 2029 NFL Draft by the Bucs. He did not see the field until week 14 when starting quarterback Patrick Mahomes was placed on injured reserve.

Washington Senators

2030

Peters fractured his foot on the opening drive against the Jacksonville Jaguars and had to sit out for the remainder of the game. J.C. Randolph came in as relief for the injured Peters. It was later revealed that Peters was going to have to sit out for 6 additional games. During the Peters' benching all but one game were decided by a single score. Peters made his return in the week 7 home game against the New Orleans Saints.
