The 2021-22 was the 2nd season of the franchise in the American Basketball Association, and the second season under head coach Milan Mack. The Bullets improved on their 15-2 record from the previous season, going 79-2, and finishing first in the Atlantic Division and in the Eastern Conference for the second straight season.
